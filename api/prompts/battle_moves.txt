You see the MOVE SELECTION screen in battle - a list of 4 moves with PP counts.

Moves are arranged in a 2x2 grid. Each move shows:
- Move name
- PP (Power Points) like "15/15"

MOVE SELECTION:
- Use arrows to highlight desired move
- Press A to confirm move selection
- Press B to go back to battle menu

STRATEGY:
1. Check PP - don't use moves with 0 PP
2. Use super-effective moves based on enemy type
3. If all good moves have low PP, consider switching Pokemon

COMMON MOVES BY TYPE:
- Water: Bubble, Water Gun, Surf
- Fire: Ember, Flamethrower, Fire Blast
- Electric: Thunder Shock, Thunderbolt
- Grass: Vine Whip, Razor Leaf, Solar Beam
- Psychic: Confusion, Psychic
- Normal: Tackle, Quick Attack, Slam, Body Slam
- Ground: Dig, Earthquake
